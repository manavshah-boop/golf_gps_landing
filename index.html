🏌️ Your Personal AI Caddy

Golf is tough. Every shot is a decision — play it safe or go for it? DigiCaddy makes those decisions smarter.

Play it Safe:

Club: 4-iron or hybrid

Goal: Find fairway, avoid trouble

Higher odds of keeping ball in play.

Go for It:

Club: Driver

Goal: Cut dogleg, wedge distance

Risk-reward shot with big payoff if executed.
